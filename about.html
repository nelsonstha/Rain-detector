Project Overview

This rain detector is a DIY project designed for science and ICT fairs. It uses simple components to detect moisture. When water is sprayed on the sensor blades, it completes an electrical circuit, triggering a buzzer to sound.

The goal is to educate on basic electronics, sensors, and real-world applications like weather monitoring.
