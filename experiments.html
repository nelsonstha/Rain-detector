Interesting Experiments to Try

1. Different Liquids Test

Test how well the detector works with different liquids:

  • Tap water (should work well)
  • Distilled water (very weak or no reaction)
  • Salt water (stronger reaction)
  • Sugar water
  • Lemon juice
  • Alcohol / sanitizer

Question to investigate: How does conductivity affect the buzzer sound?

2. Sensitivity Distance Test

Change the gap between the two metal blades (1 mm, 3 mm, 5 mm, 10 mm) and spray the same amount of water.

Measure: How wide can the gap be before the detector stops working reliably?

3. Water Quantity / Droplet Test

Use a dropper or spray bottle:

  • 1 drop
  • 3 drops
  • Continuous spray

Observe how the buzzer behaves (continuous sound vs. intermittent).

4. Add an LED Indicator
